    My dream of becoming a Farm house owner

    Reasons

    I've always dreamed of owning a farmhouse—a place where life moves at its own peaceful rhythm, far from the noise and rush of the city. I picture myself waking up to the soft sounds of birds, sunlight spilling through rustic windows, and the scent of fresh air and earth all around me.

    The idea of having land to care for, maybe a small garden with vegetables I grow myself, fruit trees, and even a few animals—chickens clucking in the morning or a dog running through the fields—feels deeply fulfilling. I want to create a home that's simple, cozy, and connected to nature. A porch to sit on with a cup of coffee, watching the seasons change. A kitchen that smells like fresh herbs and home-cooked meals. A place where time slows down.

    More than just a house, this dream is about a way of life. One that’s quiet but rich in meaning. A space where I can feel free, grounded, and truly at home.
