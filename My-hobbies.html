Swimming

Swimming is more than just a way to stay active—it's my escape, my reset button. The moment I step into the water, everything else fades away. I love the rhythm of each stroke, the calm of being submerged, and the feeling of gliding effortlessly through the water. Whether I’m swimming laps in a pool or diving into a lake on a summer morning, it gives me a unique sense of peace and focus.

It’s a full-body workout, but it never feels like a chore. I enjoy pushing myself with different strokes—freestyle when I want speed, breaststroke when I want control, and backstroke when I need to unwind. There's also something meditative about the sound of bubbles and the steady beat of my breath underwater.
